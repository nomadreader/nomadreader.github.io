About · 소개

I am a Biostatistician (PhD) working on statistics, experiment design, and causal inference. Outside of research, I am a devoted tea drinker — particularly of pu-erh — and spend a lot of time tasting, studying, and writing about tea.

This site is a personal archive of my notes across several areas. All content is written in Korean, English, or both.

Research interests: causal inference · experiment design · statistical methodology
